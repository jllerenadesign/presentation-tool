High priority · Problem 04

Photos cause the page to jump as they load

Without declared dimensions, the browser doesn't reserve space for images. Content loads first, then photos pop in and push everything down — including the "Apply" button the buyer was about to tap.

Fix: Add width and height attributes to every image tag. The browser pre-reserves the correct space before the image downloads. One-line fix per image.

The opportunity

What Sakani gains by fixing images

🚀

3× faster page load on mobile

WebP + lazy loading cuts total image weight from ~15MB to under 600KB. Pages load in under 2 seconds on a standard Saudi 4G connection.

📈

Higher rankings in Google Search

LCP, CLS, and INP are official Google ranking signals. Passing all three moves listing pages up in mobile search results — where property searches happen.

🖼️

Listing photos in Google Image Search

With alt text, property photos become discoverable in Google Images — a free traffic channel currently receiving zero benefit from Sakani's listing catalogue.

💰

Lower CDN and hosting costs

Serving 95% smaller images reduces bandwidth costs across thousands of daily listing page views — a meaningful operational saving at platform scale.

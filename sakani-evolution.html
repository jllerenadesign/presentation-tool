03 — Area 1

Discoverability

Strengthen how Sakani is presented across the Apple App Store and Google Play Store so the product is easier to find and better reflects its current value.

What can evolve
  • Refresh app store screenshots
  • Review naming and keyword strategy
  • Reassess category alignment
  • Improve the store description and bilingual positioning
Expected outcome
  • Stronger first impression
  • Better visibility in app store search
  • Clearer positioning versus competitors
What needs alignment
  • Product and Marketing ownership
  • Content preparation
  • Agreement on language and positioning

Why it matters — The app store is often the first product impression. Clearer positioning can improve discoverability, install conversion, and market perception.

04 — Area 2

User Communication

Evolve user communication from mainly transactional messages into a clearer product introduction and engagement journey.

What can evolve
  • Improve OTP SMS and email experience
  • Introduce a welcome message after signup
  • Add early product education for new users
  • Use selective communication for major features and updates
Expected outcome
  • Better first impression
  • Stronger early engagement
  • Improved awareness of Sakani's core value
What needs alignment
  • Coordination between Product and Marketing
  • Content ownership
  • Decision on which communications matter most

Why it matters — The first messages users receive shape trust, clarity, and product understanding.

05 — Area 3

Product Visibility

Make major product improvements more visible through Sakani's owned channels, especially the website and targeted user communication.

What can evolve
  • Introduce a product updates or What's New area
  • Turn banners into useful destinations
  • Publish selected feature updates on the website
  • Support major launches with SEO-friendly content
Expected outcome
  • Greater awareness of product improvements
  • Higher adoption of major features
  • Better SEO support through richer content
What needs alignment
  • Website content ownership
  • Collaboration between Product and Marketing
  • Prioritization of major updates worth communicating

Why it matters — New capabilities only create value when users know they exist. Stronger visibility can improve adoption and strengthen how the product is perceived.

06 — Area 4

Release Readiness

Improve how teams prepare for launches so functionality and experience can be validated with greater confidence before release.

What can evolve
  • Give teams earlier visibility of upcoming releases
  • Create a readiness window before launch
  • Add experience validation alongside functional testing
  • Align Product, Design, Engineering, and QA earlier
Expected outcome
  • Better launch preparedness
  • Stronger alignment across teams
  • Reduced risk of design and experience gaps
What needs alignment
  • Shared visibility of release timing
  • Clear checkpoints before launch
  • Agreement on who validates what and when

Why it matters — Stronger preparation improves launch quality, reduces visible gaps, and gives teams time to validate what will be released.

07 — Next Step

Recommended Next Step

Select one focused area, confirm ownership, and define a practical first scope with the relevant stakeholders.

Option 1
User Communication

The opportunity is clear, the improvement can be visible quickly, and it strengthens the connection between Product and Marketing.

  • OTP communication improvement
  • Welcome email for new users
  • One follow-up communication introducing a key capability
Option 2
Release Readiness

Addresses a real execution need, improves preparedness across teams, and helps reduce gaps before launch.

  • Pre-release readiness checklist
  • Shared visibility of launch timing
  • Experience validation checkpoint before release
08 — Closing

Sakani's next stage of evolution is not only about adding more features. It is also about making product value more visible, easier to understand, and more consistently delivered.

The opportunity now is to align on one focused area, confirm ownership, and move forward with a practical first scope.
